I'm a third-year undergraduate student at the University of Maryland, College Park, pursing a degree in journalism and government and politics with a minor in geographic information science.

When I'm not in class, you can likely find me in The Diamondback's newsroom, where I work as editor in chief to oversee more than 100 staff members of my university's independent student newspaper. I make long-standing editorial decisions about our coverage and policies and edit news, sports, opinion, data, multimedia and arts and culture stories five nights a week.

I most recently was a reporting intern for The Frederick News-Post through the Maryland-Delaware-D.C. Press Association's internship program. I covered a variety of breaking news, enterprise and data-driven stories across Frederick County.

Last summer, I was an intern for Aviation Week in Washington, D.C., reporting on aviation, aerospace and defense. I covered topics such as India's successful 2023 moon mission, the fiscal 2024 national defense policy bill, workforce development in the space industry and scientific missions on Mars.

I'm working toward a career in data and computational journalism. I code in R, Python, HTML and CSS, analyze data in Google Sheets/Excel and make visualizations using Datawrapper and Flourish. I'm currently learning ArcGIS and QGIS and teaching myself JavaScript. I also coded and designed this website!
